Do you know how to identify who is Pikachu referring to?

Normally most of the Pokemons can only say their name. But Pikachu says his name in different ways to call everyone. If you hear it properly, you will notice these followings used by Pikachu.

1. Pikapi : When referring to Ash.
2. Pikachu-Pi : When referring to Misty.
3. Pika-Chu : When referring to Brock or Axew.
4. Pikaka : When referring to Dawn.
5. PiPiPi : When referring to Togepi.
6. PikakaPika : When referring to Bulbasaur.
7. PikaPika : When referring to Squirtle, Staraptor, Infernape or Palpitode.
8. PiPi-kachu : When referring to Ream Rocket.
9. Pi-Pikachu : Pikachu says this to Ash when he won a badge, or catches a new Pokemon or anything similar.
10. Pika-Pikachu : He says this when referring to himself.
11. Chaa : Anything he says happily or excitedly.
